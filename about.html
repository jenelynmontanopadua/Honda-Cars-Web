EVOLUTION

A New Era of Innovation

In 1986, Honda made a historic move by launching the Acura brand, becoming the first Japanese automaker to enter the luxury vehicle market. This decision reshaped global perceptions of Honda, proving that the company’s engineering excellence extended beyond efficiency and reliability into high-performance and premium design. The introduction of Acura demonstrated Honda’s ability to blend innovation, comfort, and precision, setting new standards for luxury automobiles. This vision reached a defining moment in 1990 with the debut of the legendary NSX supercar, a vehicle that revolutionized the sports car world by combining supercar performance with everyday usability. Today, Honda stands at the forefront of automotive innovation, continually adapting to the changing needs of drivers around the world.
